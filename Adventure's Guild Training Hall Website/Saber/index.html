Saber

HEMA saber fencing is the study of real historical cut‑and‑thrust swordplay with a single‑handed saber, based on authentic European military and dueling manuals. Instead of sport rules that limit how you can move or strike, you learn how sabers were actually used: dynamic footwork, powerful cuts, precise thrusts, and adaptive defense against both blade and body targets. Training blends technical drills, tactical exercises, and controlled sparring with masks and protective gear, building timing, distance management, and sharp decision‑making under pressure. It feels fast, fluid, and athletic—like a duel out of a Napoleonic battlefield or 19th‑century fencing hall—while still emphasizing safety, control, and respect for your training partners.
