Longsword

HEMA longsword fencing is the art of fighting with the classic two‑handed medieval sword, reconstructed from real historical combat manuals. Students learn how to move, cut, thrust, and defend using the same principles and techniques that knights and fencing masters once relied on, adapted for modern safety with masks and protective gear. It combines the precision of fencing, the strategy of chess, and the athleticism of a combat sport, with a strong emphasis on control, respect, and technical skill rather than brute force. Whether you’re drawn by history, dueling, or just the idea of mastering a longsword like the heroes of legend, HEMA longsword offers a deep, rewarding path to train both body and mind.
